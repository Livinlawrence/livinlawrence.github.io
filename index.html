Kagazz

Kagazz was conceived to save time and money by eliminating the stacks of paper receipts that pile up on your desk. Kagazz converts paper receipts into a digital format, capturing the key data from each receipt. This enables paperless accounting, which can be accessed virtually anywhere through a readily available cloud central storage provider. Kagazz seamlessly integrates with many of the top accounting packages, to automatically sync receipts and expenses data. This eliminates double inputting and ensures an efficient merger of expenses data with you're accounting department.
